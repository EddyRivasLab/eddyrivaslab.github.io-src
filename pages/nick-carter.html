Nick Carter

Nick Carter is a staff member in the Eddy Lab. He is a lead developer on the HMMER software package. Prior to joining the Eddy Lab, Nick worked in computer architecture research at Intel and as an Assistant Professor at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign.
